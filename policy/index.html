Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how telegram-jobs ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Telegram bot and related services (the Service).

Project: telegram-jobs Contact: 2824988@ucm.sk

Who This Service Is For

The Service is intended for Telegram channel owners, creators, and administrators who manage job channels and want to automate job collection, moderation, and publishing.

Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following data:

  • Telegram account data: user ID, username, display name, and chat/channel identifiers.
  • Content data: messages, commands, and job post data submitted to or processed by the bot.
  • Operational data: timestamps, moderation actions, queue status, publishing history, and delivery results.
  • Technical data: error logs, diagnostic logs, and basic usage metadata needed for reliability and security.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data unless you provide it in content submitted to the bot.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate core bot features, including parsing, queueing, moderation, and publishing.
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot technical issues.
  • Improve performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations where applicable.

Legal Basis Where Applicable

If GDPR or similar laws apply, we process data based on:

  • Performance of a service requested by the user.
  • Legitimate interests, including security, maintenance, and improvement.
  • Legal obligations.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

We may share limited data only when necessary with:

  • Infrastructure and service providers under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal authorities, if required by law or valid legal process.
  • A successor entity in case of merger, acquisition, or asset transfer.

Data Retention

We keep data only as long as needed for operational purposes, moderation history, security, and legal compliance. When data is no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it within a reasonable period.

Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

International Transfers

If data is processed outside your country, we take reasonable steps to ensure appropriate safeguards consistent with applicable privacy laws.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your data.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Request data portability.
To exercise these rights, contact us at 2824988@ucm.sk.

Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13, or a higher age required by local law. If we learn we collected data from a child in violation of law, we will delete it promptly.

Third-Party Platforms

This Service runs on Telegram and may rely on third-party infrastructure. Their data practices are governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will publish the updated version with a new effective date.
